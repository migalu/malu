Development of the Internet in China

2.Semester, personal

Description

Data visualization, the project requires no numbers, no figurative icons, only through shapes, colors, logic, and interactive behavior to interpret the change of data. I chose the topic, development of the Internet in China from 2000 to 2010.

Research

After I collect and analyze large amounts of data, I chose three representative data, the number of Internet users, number of pages and the Internet value. The final data structure is:

Internet users: man, woman
Number of pages: Company, trade, personal, government and education, other
Value: online shopping, online advertising, online games, mobile Internet, other

Shape

Based on several features of an equilateral triangle, I chose this graph to show the data. Fixed proportion of high and edges is suitable for data. I have data pf six years. Each corner of an equilateral triangle is 60 degrees. So data can be rotated for each year. Six sets of data can be compared exactly 360 degrees around. By the way, the moral of the triangle contains a high-tech and futuristic.

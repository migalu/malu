Cocktail Genius

2.Semester, teamwork

Description

Such APP is applied for quick order in bar, which is directly connected to cocktail mixer to produce tasty cocktails according to operating point. The user could freely search for different cocktails due to different taste, colour and theme. Besides, such APP could also be customized to change each recipe as guests have different preferences. On the page of DIY, the visualized bartending element gives more direct manipulations which could be saved as a favourite name after. In addition, with the help of mark, history record and ranking list the searching results could be more rapidly obtained by the user.

Dancing Robot

2.Semester, teamwork

Description

According to the schematics of electrical wirings for all of the wiring connections, the Arduino was utilised to control 5 components. After that, we continue to complete the programming in the processing stage, utilising the keyboard to control the direction and motion of each component. Motivated by the dancing robot, we completed the dancing mechanical arm, where each part of the mechanical arm will be able to function accordingly according to the changes in rhythm and strength of music.
